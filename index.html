What Actually Drives You?

Most people can name their values. Fewer people understand how those values shape their decisions, especially when two good things are in tension with each other. This tool helps you see that clearly.

How It Works

You will read 27 short scenarios. Each one puts two values in tension. There are no wrong answers. You are simply choosing which pull is stronger for you in that moment.

Your results map 18 core human values organized into 9 natural opposing pairs. The insight comes from seeing your values relative to each other, not from any single score being high or low.

A note before you begin. This is a self-reflection tool designed to spark insight and conversation. It is not a psychological assessment, a diagnostic instrument, or a substitute for professional counseling. There are no right or wrong profiles. Your responses are not stored, saved, or shared with anyone. Results are generated in your browser and disappear when you close the page. Use what resonates, set aside what does not, and bring anything interesting to your next coaching conversation.
